Our Story

Built From a Simple Frustration

RentLease started in 2023 when our founders struggled to find a reliable place to rent equipment for a short-term project — and realized thousands of others faced the same problem across property, vehicles, furniture, and electronics.

Today, we've grown into a trusted global marketplace connecting verified owners with renters in over 120 cities, built on transparency, safety, and simplicity at every step.

Get In Touch

Our Mission

To make renting and leasing as simple, safe, and accessible as buying — so people choose access over ownership without compromise.

Our Vision

A world where every idle asset — a home, a car, a tool — finds someone who needs it, reducing waste and creating opportunity for owners everywhere.
